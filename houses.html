Hogwarts Houses

  H u f f l e p u f f

Hufflepuff Emblem- The Badger is the symbol of Hufflepuff house, the official house colours are Yellow and Black.

Hufflepuff House Ghost- Each Hogwarts House has its own house ghost, Which acts as a mascot for the house. Hufflepuff's ghost is the Fat Friar.

Traits of a Hufflepuff Student- Hufflepuff Students are most likely an incredibly hardworking, loyal and trustworthy person. They are known to have an extremely strong moral compass, which is probably why Hufflepuff has produced the least number of dark witches/wizards.

  G r y f f i n d o r

Gryffindor Emblem- The Gryffindor emblem is a lion, as it is widely believed to be the bravest of all creatures. The official Gryffindor house colours are scarlet and gold.

Gryffindor House Ghost- Each Hogwarts House has its own house ghost, Which acts as a mascot for the house. Gryffindor's ghost is Sir Nicholas De Mimsy Porpington, otherwise known as Nearly Headless Nick.

Traits of a Gryffindor Student- Gryffindor has reputation for producing heroic and noble witches/wizards, however not all Gryffindor Students are good. If you find yourself in Gryffindor, it is likely because you display traits like courage, bravery and determination. Gryffindor Students are known for standing up for what they believe in, even if it means breaking the rules.

  R a v e n c l a w

Ravenclaw Emblem- The Ravenclaw emblem is an eagle. The official Ravenclaw house colours are Blue and Bronze.

Ravenclaw House Ghost- Each Hogwarts House has its own house ghost, Which acts as a mascot for the house. Ravenclaw's ghost is the Grey Lady, otherwise known as Helena Ravenclaw.

Traits of a Ravenclaw Student- Ravenclaw Stduents are most likely a brainy, handworking and academically motivated individual. Ravenclaw students are known to be the smartest that Hogwarts has offer.

  S l y t h e r i n

Slytherin Emblem- The Slytherin emblem is a green serpent. The official house colours are Emerald Green and Silver.

Slytherin House Ghost- Each Hogwarts House has its own house ghost, Which acts as a mascot for the house. Slytherin's ghost is the Bloody Baron.

Traits of a Slytherin Student- Ravenclaw Stduents has a reputation for producing dark witches and wizards, although its important to not that more than a few heroic witches/wizards have been produced by Slytherin. Mostly Slytherin Students are Ambitious, cunning, leadership and resoucefulness.
